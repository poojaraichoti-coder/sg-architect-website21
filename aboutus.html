About Us

**Sushil Gupta Architect** is a professional architectural firm located at 2/373, Vivek Khand, Gomti Nagar, Lucknow.

Sushil Gupta Architect is a professional architecture and design studio based in Vivek Khand, Gomti Nagar, Lucknow. We specialize in delivering creative, functional and modern architectural solutions for residential, commercial and interior projects. Our focus is simple — to provide smart planning, elegant designs and high-quality project execution. From house maps to complete architectural planning and elevation design, we offer end-to-end architectural services tailored to each client’s needs. With a commitment to innovation, accuracy and client satisfaction, we ensure every project is designed with attention to detail and delivered with professionalism. At Sushil Gupta Architect Company, we believe in creating spaces that are beautiful, practical and built to enhance everyday living.

Founder, Sushil Gupta Architect Master’s Scholar in Urban Design | Design Visionary

The founder of Sushil Gupta Architect is a forward-thinking design professional with a strong academic foundation in Urban Design, having successfully completed his Master’s degree in Urban Design. With years of hands-on professional experience, he brings a refined blend of creativity, strategic thinking, and technical excellence to every project he undertakes. Founded in 2016, Sushil Gupta Architect was established with a clear vision to deliver timeless architectural solutions that seamlessly balance aesthetics, functionality, and environmental responsibility. Since its inception, the firm has remained dedicated to redefining urban spaces through innovative concepts, precision-driven planning, and a client-centric approach. Under his leadership, the practice emphasizes thoughtful design, meticulous attention to detail, and a deep understanding of human-centered environments. His design philosophy is rooted in the belief that architecture should not only inspire visually but also enhance the way people live, work, and interact with their surroundings. With a strong focus on quality, integrity, and innovation, he continues to guide the firm toward creating meaningful, sustainable, and future-ready spaces.
